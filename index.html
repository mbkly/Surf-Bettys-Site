Surf Bettys

The Ultimate Surf Bettys was founded in 2013, by Ultimate Surf N Skate in Auckland, New Zealand. We have been the go-to place for Bettys for 8 years and counting for amazing deals and products paired with genuine advice. As a loyalty program, Ultimate Surf N Skate offer Bettys a 10% discount off the RRP on wetsuits and ALL surf hardware. Skate gear included. This group was originally set up to help female surfers in Auckland, network and share surf experiences. Ultimate Surf N Skate Shop staff will post up weekly surf reports (Auckland region) and help you find the best surf available; we are happy to help with any technical questions on surf or skate gear.
